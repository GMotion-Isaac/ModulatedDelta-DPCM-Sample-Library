# How to get your MD Library ID
Your MD Library ID is a unique ID used to organize your submissions into your own folder inside the library. This keeps all your samples together and protects your privacy if you don't want to use your real name.

## How to get your ID
1. Go to the **Issues** tab in this repo.
2. Open a new issue with the title: `Requesting creation of a new MD Library ID - [YourUsernameorAlias]`.
3. A library contributor will reply to your issue with your unique ID, For example: `MDID-CD1301`
4. Once you get your ID, you can start submitting your samples!

## Why do we use IDs?
* **Organization:** All your samples will live inside your personal folder. A new folder with your ID is created, and each sample category with the preset number will get its own folder inside it.
* **Privacy:** If your username includes sensitive data like real names, it will be replaced with your MD Library ID to keep your info safe. You can also just use aliases.
